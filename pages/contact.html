Frequently Asked Questions

What are your membership plans?

We offer Basic ($29/month), Premium ($59/month), and Elite ($99/month) plans. Each includes different levels of access to our facilities and services.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year at no additional cost. Contact our support team to arrange this.

Do you offer a trial period?

Yes! We offer a free 7-day trial for new members. This includes full access to our facilities and one complimentary group class.

How do I book a personal trainer?

You can book a personal trainer through our mobile app, website, or by speaking directly with our front desk staff.
